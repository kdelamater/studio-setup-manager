Creative Technique Prep

Needed? Technique Prep Notes
Stencils / Mylar
Requests for stencils to be cut on plotter must be assigned to studio assistants 2 weeks in advance of date needed. Please upload the assignment and files to AirTable. It is assumed all stencils will require Crystal Clear on each side. Please note if you will need a carrier sheet and if so what size.
Silkscreen
Each collaborator is individually responsible for coordinating fabrication of silkscreens with the support of studio assistants. Requests for silkscreen fabrication should be sent to the vendor at least 2 weeks before they are needed.
Casting Setup
Please specify any additional supplies needed for casting. This might include mold release, pellon strips, rocks, and wire carts for drying.
Vinyl / Watermarks
Requests for vinyl stencils to be cut on plotter must be assigned to studio assistants 2 weeks in advance of date needed. Please upload the assignment and files to AirTable. It is assumed all stencils will be weeded and covered with transfer tape unless otherwise instructed.
